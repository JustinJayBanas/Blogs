Week 1 of February

Day 1: Our internship began with an orientation led by the dean. Ate Ella guided us to our assigned department, BU-USDS, where we met our mentor. Our team has two IT students and one CBEM student. Our mentor explained our tasks, which include fixing their system and adding new features.
Day 2: We helped renovate tables and organize documents in the office. After that, we started checking the spreadsheet monitoring system to find what needed fixing.
Day 3: There was a power outage, so Sir Ronaldo gave me clerical work. I checked documents to make sure they were complete and organized.

Week 2 of February

We worked on the spreadsheet monitoring system and used Google Apps Script to fix errors and make it work better. The BU-USDS team tested it and said it was faster and had fewer mistakes.

Week 3 of February

The spreadsheet system was slow, so we used Google Apps Script to make it faster. We also added a simple dashboard to show important data. The BU-USDS team liked it because it was easy to use and helped them check information quickly.

Week 4 of February

We decided to stop using the spreadsheet and started building a web-based monitoring system. Our mentor agreed it was a better idea. We made a simple prototype with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, connected to a database. The BU-USDS team liked the prototype and told us to keep going.
